About Abode

ANEIGHBORHOOD  KITCHEN & BAR, IN OAKMONT.

Brick walls, tall windows, big plates. Brunch on weekends, dinner soon.

Brick walls. Tall windows. A bar at the back. We took a corner on Allegheny and built a kitchen and bar inside it — the kind of room where you stay for a second cup, or a third pour.

Brunch is country-fried wagyu next to cereal-milk french toast. Cold-pressed juice, an espresso, a bourbon if you’re inclined. Real food, plated like we mean it.

Dinner service follows soon — same idea, dimmer lights, longer pours.
